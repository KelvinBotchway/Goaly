Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: How do I create a new goal?
A: Navigate to your dashboard and click on "Add New Goal." Fill in the required details, set milestones, and click "Save."

Q: Can I edit or update my goals?
A: Yes, on your dashboard, select the goal you wish to update. Click on the "Edit" option, make changes, and save.

Q: Is my goal progress visible to others?
A: By default, your goals are private. However, you can choose to share them with team members for collaborative efforts.

For further assistance or inquiries, please reach out to our support team at support@goaly.com. We're here to ensure your experience with Goaly is seamless and successful.
